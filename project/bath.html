Bath & Stonehenge

About Bath

Bath is a city located in the county of Somerest, England. It is knowned for its Roman-built baths. Many visitors came here for a one-day tour of Bath and the Stonehenge. I think it is pretty cool to see the roman baths built hundreds years ago.

The Roman Baths

To enter the Roman Baths, you have to buy the ticket first. Inside the building, you can see many handicrafts made hundreds years ago. You can also get an audio guide, which will tell you the history of the Roman Baths in detail.

Way to the Stonehenge

I am still in doubt whether the Stonehenge is built by human or by aliens. Originally, I thought the piles of the stones would be gigantic; however, it did not turned out as what I've been imagining for the whole time. It's smaller in size, but it's still great to see this wonder in person. By the way, you have to take a shuttle bus to arrive the Stonehenge. There are many hays on the side of the road.
